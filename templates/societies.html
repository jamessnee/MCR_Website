Societies

From charity to rock climbing and everything in between, Christ's College is home to a large number of clubs and societies run by students. In addition to the range of clubs and societies in College, there are many sports teams and societies with members from around the University. More details can be found on the CUSU Societies Directory. Often, but by no means always, college-level sports will be more relaxed and friendly, whereas university-level teams will require more dedication.

Below is a list of College societies for 2013-2014 with contact details. If you are involved in running a College club or society which is missing from this list, please email the liaison officer with your details to be added.
