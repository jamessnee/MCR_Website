Alex Litvinenko

Swaps Officer

I talk with the MCRs of other colleges, both Cambridge and Oxford, and organise formal swaps, where a group of Christ's students goes to another college for dinner in their hall (often with pre- and post-drinks!) and the other college does the same on a different day. This means that Christ's students have a chance to experience what formals in other colleges are liks and get to hang out with people from outside on the return swaps as well.
